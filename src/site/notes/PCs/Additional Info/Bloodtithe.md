> [!error]- Bloodtithe
> Each battle while staggered. when you first take damage that as a type (acid, cold, fire, etc.), you deal [2] damage of that type with weapon attacks for the rest of the battle (champion: +2d6; epic: +3d6).

> [!warning]- Curse
> When you roll a natural 1 (on any d20 roll), you take double the weapon's extra damage. 

> [!Info] Quirk
> The grass is always greener. (Your regrets and lusts are amplified)

"I will slice all who stand in your path... do you accept?"

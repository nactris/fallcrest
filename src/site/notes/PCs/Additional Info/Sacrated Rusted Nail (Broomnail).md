Ordinary old rusty nail with runic markings. Violently repels demons, to the point of forcefully extracting possessing demons from their hosts. 

Demons seem to actively avoid it. Given to [[PCs/Zimriel Kachaçe-Queensward\|Zimriel Kachaçe-Queensward]] by [[Characters/Lucian Grimhollow\|Lucian Grimhollow]].

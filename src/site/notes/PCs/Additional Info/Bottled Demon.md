Demonic red mist that can possess people. It enters through the wounds or openings (such as mouth). 

Managed to possess [[Characters/Diz Woone\|Diz Woone]], kill several [[PCs/Additional Info/Chickens\|chickens]], force [[Characters/bLuEhAt15\|bLuEhAt15]] to fight [[PCs/Mira\|Mira]] and [[PCs/Yonder\|Yonder]].
Soundly defeated, captured into several jars and magical sealed vials (Mira had them apparently).

Feisty, tries to escape the jars constantly. "Glares" from inside and moves around constantly.
Apparently summoned by [[Crusaider\|Crusaider's]] people. They were supposed to make [[PCs/Yonder\|Yonder]] kill [[Characters/Lady Allande\|Lady Allande]]. Possibly to further torture [[Characters/Lord Warden Aurelio Markelhay\|Lord Warden]].

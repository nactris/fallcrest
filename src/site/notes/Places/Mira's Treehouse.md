#home
Small treehouse built by Mira on a big tree just outside Fallcrest. 
Small but cozy. Well insulated and covered from floor to ceiling in various trinkets, polished coins pieces of glass and other shinnies. Floor and walls are covered with blankets so its more like a squirrel's hole or magpie's nest.

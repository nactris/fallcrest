#home
Small treehouse built by [[PCs/Mira\|Mira]] on a big tree just outside [[Places/Fallcrest\|Fallcrest]] even more to the west than [[Places/Yondersnap Homestead\|Yondersnap Homestead]] is. 
Small but cosy. Well insulated and covered from floor to ceiling in various trinkets, polished coins, pieces of glass and other shinnies. Floor and walls are covered with blankets so its more like a squirrel's hole or magpie's nest.

#npc 
Chickens are good targets, hates trees, they are huge, hates music it gets stuck in his head.
Kid, apprentice of [[Characters/Fallcrest/Nimozaran the Green\|Nimozaran the Green]]. 
Didn't pass the magic exam. 
In deep awe of [[PCs/Gingersnap Myra\|Ginger's]] necromancy. Will probably try to learn magic from grandma.
Self-taught, despite having a master.

Has a 'quirk', he cannot really affect magically stuff over 5 feet from him.
He is a craven mage or lantern mage (a coward mage, contendingly timid - book definition).
Because of that he is best suited to be a [magic swordsman](https://www.13thagesrd.com/classes/3rd-party-classes/swordmage/).

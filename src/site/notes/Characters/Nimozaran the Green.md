#npc
an elderly wizard who was once apprenticed to the last of the old guild mages. Old Nim is the only guild wizard left who didn’t perish or flee during the war. Nimozaran considers himself the “High Septarch of Fallcrest” and master of the guild, whose membership now includes only himself and a rather unpromising male halfling apprentice named [[Characters/Little Tobolar 'Toblerone or Toby' Quickfoot\|Tobolar Quickfoot]].

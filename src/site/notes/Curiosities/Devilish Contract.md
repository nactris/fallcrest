This contract is supposedly backed by an Archdevil by the name of Ballzebul, Lord of the Seventh Maladomini. It promises protection from all the forces of hell by their great authority to Gingersnap, the [[Characters/Lord Warden Aurelio Markelhay\|Lord Warden]], and the Lady Allande as well as the city of Fallcrest for 777,777 years during which time the soul of Marsalis Markelhay is "at the pleasure of the Archduke of Maladomini and his vassals". Any adjustments to this contract are to be petitioned in person with Neabaz, Herald of Lies in the city of Grenpoli.
